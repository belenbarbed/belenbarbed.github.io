Carrot Cake

Ingredients (for a 15cm pan):

  • 350g grated carrots
  • 160ml cooking oil
  • 150g brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g flour
  • 2tsp baking powder
  • 1/2tsp cinnamon powder
  • Sprinkle of salt
  • 250g cream cheese, for the icing
  • 120g icing sugar, for the icing
  • 100ml double cream, for the icing

Method:

  1. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  2. In another bowl, mix together the oil and sugars. Add in eggs one at time and mix thoroughly.
  3. Add flour mixture to wet ingredients in 2 portions, mixing inbetween.
  4. Fold in carrots and pecans.
  5. Pour into a lightly greased pan and bake at 175 for 50 mins, until a toothpick comes out clean from the middle.
  6. Whisk the three icing incredients: cream cheese, icing sugar, and double cream.
  7. Let the cake cool, cut in half vertically, then decorate with icing.
