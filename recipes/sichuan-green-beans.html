Sichuan-style Green Beans - source

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 100g minced pork
  • 200g fine green beans
  • 1 tbsp spicy broadbean paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp shaosing wine
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Method:

  1. Dry fry the green beans in a wok wihout oil until soft and slightly charred, like 20 mins.
  2. Set the beans aside.
  3. Cook the minced pork and chopped garlic in a bit of sesame oil until it starts turning brown.
  4. Add the spicy broadbean paste, the Shaosing wine, and soy sauce, cook until combined.
  5. Turn the heat off and add the beans back in.
  6. Mix and serve with rice.
