Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup

Ingredients (for 4):

  • 800g of beef (roasting cut like brisket)
  • 1 thumb of ginger, smashed
  • 4 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • 3 green onions
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 dried chillies
  • 1/2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp spicy bean paste (dubanjiang)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • Chinese aromatics (2 star anise, 1/2 cinnamon stick, 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns, 1/2 tbsp each fennel, coriander and cumin seeds)
  • 4 portions of fresh wheat noodles
  • 2 bok choi
  • coriander
  • pickled mustard greens

Method:

  1. Boil the diced beef for 1min, then thoroughly rinse to remove impurities.
  2. Saute the ginger, garlic, green onions and onion until the latter is translucent.
  3. Add the tomato and dried chillies ripped in half.
  4. Add the meat, tomato paste, doubanjiang, soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and sugar. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Pour 950ml of water into the pot, as well as all the spice aromatics.
  6. Set the Instant Pot to 1h40mins on the stew setting (not venting), or simmer in a regular pot for 3h.
  7. When that's done, release the IP valve
  8. Boil the noodles, adding the bok choi to the pot for the final 2 mins.
  9. Pour the soup over the noodles and serve with coriander and picked mustard greens.
