Chicken Udon Soup

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 1L dashi broth
  • 30ml soy sauce
  • 30ml mirin
  • 30ml sake
  • 15ml sugar
  • 1 leek
  • 1 green onion
  • 2 boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 portions of udon noodles (frozen or dry)

Method:

  1. Make the dashi broth (mix 3 teaspoons of powder with 1L of water).
  2. Add the soy sauce, mirin and sugar to the stock. Let boil for 2-3 mins.
  3. Cut the leek length-wise and clean between the leaves. Cut length-wise again and then into 5cm pieces.
  4. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces
  5. Add the leek, chicken, and sake to the soup.
  6. Bring the soup back to a boil, then cook until the chicken is fully cooked and the leek is tender.
  7. If the noodles are not fresh, cook separately as the chicken is cooking and then add. If they're fresh, add them directly to the soup now.
  8. Sprinkle chopped green onion and serve.
