Ensaladilla Rusa

Ingredients (for 4):

  • 2-3 potatoes (450 g)
  • 4 carrots
  • 2 eggs
  • 20 olives
  • 3 tbsp peas
  • 200 g canned tuna
  • mayonnaise
  • salt
  • parsley

Method:

  1. Boil the peeled potatoes and carrots for 25 mins.
  2. Add the eggs and some salt to the boiling water.
  3. Drain everything out, and chop into small cubes once cooled.
  4. Mix it all in a bowl with the tuna, chopped olives, peas, and mayo
  5. Season to taste with salt and parsley
