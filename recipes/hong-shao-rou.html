Hong Shao Rou

Ingredients (for 4):

  • 600g pork belly, in cubes
  • ??g fried tofu (half a bag)
  • 3tbsp sugar
  • 100ml Shaoxing wine
  • 250ml water
  • 2tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 thumb of ginger, sliced
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 3 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon sticks
  • 1/2 onion

Method:

  1. Boil the pork for a couple of minutes to get rid of the scum.
  2. Add the sugar to an empty pot, and cook until it starts melting.
  3. Add the pork and coat it in the sugar until there's no more left.
  4. Add all the rest of the ingredients.
  5. Simmer for 1h covered, then for 10mins uncovered, or until the liquid has reduced entirely.
  6. Remove the bay leaves, star anise, and cinnamon sticks before serving.
