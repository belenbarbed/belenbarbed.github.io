Roscón de Reyes

Ingredients (for one 1/2kg roscón):

  • 250g plain flour
  • 60g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 25ml warm milk
  • 25ml warm water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp spiced rum
  • 1 pack active dry yeast
  • 1/2 lemon peel
  • 1/2 orange peel
  • salt
  • 60g room temperature butter
  • 1 beaten egg, for the egg wash
  • 50g minced almonds
  • 50g sugar, for the topping
  • candied fruits

Method:

  1. Mix 50g of the flour with the water and yeast. Mix into a ball and let it rise until doubled in size.
  2. Knead the rest of the flour with the sugar, egg, milk, salt, peels, vanilla, and rum.
  3. Once fully formed, add the yeast dough little by little, making sure it's all integrated.
  4. Knead further until the dough is elastic and does not stick.
  5. Let it rest until doubled in size again. You can repeat the knead-rest process a couple of times more.
  6. Pierce the centre of the dough ball and extend into a ring.
  7. Place on a baking sheet and press it down slightly. Let it rest again until doubled in size.
  8. Wash the outside with the beaten egg and sprinkle the sugar and almonds.
  9. Decorate with the candied fruit and bake for 15mins at 200ºC.
