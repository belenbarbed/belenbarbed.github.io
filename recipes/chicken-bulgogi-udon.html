Chicken Bulgogi Udon - source

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp of ginger
  • 45ml soy sauce
  • 30ml mirin
  • 15ml sugar
  • 30ml sesame oil (+ more for garnish)
  • 2 portions of udon noodles
  • half an onion, sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds
  • Black pepper

Method:

  1. Thinly slice the chicken breasts and put them in a bowl with the soy sauce, mirin, sugar, sesame oil, pepper, and grated ginger and garlic.
  2. Let marinate for at least 10 mins.
  3. Run the udon noodles under hot tap water to loosen them, rinse in cold water, drain, and set aside.
  4. Heat a large pan with sesame oil and cook the sliced onion until soft and golden.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and stir fry until the chicken is cooked.
  6. Add the noodles and stir fry together some more.
  7. Add the chopped green onions.
  8. Serve with sesame oil and sesame seeds.
