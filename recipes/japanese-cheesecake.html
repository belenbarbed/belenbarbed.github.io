Japanese Cheesecake

Ingredients (for 4):

  • 130g cream cheese
  • 30g unsaltedbutter
  • 50ml milk
  • 30g flour
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 5 tbsp white sugar
  • 3 eggs separated into whites and yolks

Method:

  1. Melt the cheese, butter, and milk in a bowl using bain marie.
  2. Remove from the heat, add the egg yolks and whisk together.
  3. Sift in the flour and corn starch, and whish again.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg whites to soft peaks, while adding the sugar slowly.
  5. Add 1/3 of the egg whites into the other mixture and combine well.
  6. Add the remaining egg whites gently, folding without over mixing or letting too much air out.
  7. Pour into a pan, then place it in a trough with water 5cm high.
  8. Bake for 1h at 120ºC, then for 15mins at 150ºC.
  9. Remove from the pan and let cool before serving.
