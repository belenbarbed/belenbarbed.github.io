Shortbread biscuits

Ingredients (for one ?x?cm tray):

  • 140g unsalted butter
  • 140g demerara sugar
  • 270g plain flour
  • 55g cornstarch
  • A pinch of salt
  • 55g unsalted butter, for browning
  • 4 tbsp demerara sugar, for coating

Method:

  1. Brush the baking tray with some softened butter, and sprinkle half of the coating sugar.
  2. Brown the butter in a pan, taking care not to burn the milk solids.
  3. Combine the butter, browned butter, salt, and sugar in the bowl of a stand mixer. Mix well for 2-3 mins.
  4. Meanwhile, combine the flour and cornstarch in a bowl. Whisk well to combine.
  5. Add half of the dry ingredients and continue mixing for 30s.
  6. Add the remaining dry ingredients and mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  7. Transfer the dough to the prepared pan and gently press it into the sides and corners of the tray.
  8. Top the shortbread with the rest of the coating sugar.
  9. Bake for 1h - 1.5h at 150ºC or until the top is set and has become lightly golden.
  10. Allow the shortbread to cool for 10-15 mins before cutting, and a little more before serving.
