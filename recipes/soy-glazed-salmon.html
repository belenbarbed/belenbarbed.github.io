Soy-Glazed Salmon

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 45ml soy sauce
  • 30ml water
  • 30ml lemon juice
  • 60ml brown sugar
  • 60ml olive oil

Method:

  1. Mix everything (except the salmon) to make the sauce.
  2. Coat salmon in sauce (keep excess in oven dish) and cook for 15-20 mins at 200ºC.
