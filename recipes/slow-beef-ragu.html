Slow Beef Ragu

Ingredients (for a metric fuckton):

  • 800g of beef (roasting cut like brisket)
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes (400g)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 250ml red wine
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 400ml water
  • Plenty of salt, pepper, olive oil and parsley

Method:

  1. Cut the beef into 4-6 smaller chunks. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear the beef on all sides until slightly bronwed and set aside.
  3. Re-oil the pan and sauté the diced onions and minced garlic.
  4. Add the diced carrot and celery and sauté for a further 5 mins.
  5. Add all the remaining ingredients in the pot and put the beef back too.
  6. Cover and simmer for 2h.
  7. Remove the beef and shred with 2 forks. Return the shredded beef to the pot.
  8. Continue simmering without a lid for 30mins.
  9. Taste and adjust seasoning as appropriate.
  10. Serve as sauce with pasta and parmesan on top.
