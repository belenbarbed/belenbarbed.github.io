Mapo Tofu

Ingredients (for 2-3 servings):

  • 1 thumb of ginger
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 200g minced pork
  • 1 block of silken tofu (~280g)
  • 2 tbsp Doubanjiang paste
  • a splash of Shaoxing wine
  • 200ml chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in a little water
  • vegetable oil
  • chilli oil to taste

Method:

  1. Fry off the minced garlic and ginger in a mix of vegetable and chilli oil
  2. Once fragrant, add the minced pork, and let it cook completely.
  3. Add the Shaoxing wine and Doubanjiang, and stir well.
  4. Add the chicken broth, and cut up the tofu into cubes while it simmers for a bit.
  5. Mix in the cornstarch slurry and, once thickened, add the tofu, stirring carefully.
  6. Add a bit more chilli oil and let it all cook for a further 5mins before serving.
