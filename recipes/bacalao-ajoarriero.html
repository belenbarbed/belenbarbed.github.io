Bacalao Ajoarriero

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2 cod fillets (~300g)
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 small potato
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 150g canned tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, paprika (pimentón)

Method:

  1. Chop the peppers, potato, and onions into small cubes, and mince the garlic.
  2. Shred the cod fillets as finely as possible.
  3. Fry the garlic in plenty of olive oil, and add the rest of the vegetables when it starts to turn golden.
  4. Cook for 15mins, then add the cod.
  5. Keep frying until the cod is pretty much done, then add the tomatoes.
  6. Mix thoroughly and season with the salt, pepper, and paprika.
