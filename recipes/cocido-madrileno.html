Cocido Madrileño

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2 onions, peeled
  • 4 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 2 cans of chickpeas (500g drained)
  • 2 small morcillas
  • 2 small chorizos
  • 1 jamón bone
  • 1 piece of tocino

Method:

  1. Boil the meat and bones for 10 mins and remove the generated scum. Drain.
  2. Add all the ingredients in a large pot with enough water to cover them all.
  3. Simmer for 3h.
  4. Add the short noodles for the final 10 mins.
  5. After removing from the heat, take the meats out, chop them roughly, and add them back in.
  6. Stir through and serve.
