Tortilla de Patatas

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 4 eggs
  • 400g potatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 300ml olive oil at least
  • Salt, pepper

Method:

  1. Peel, wash, dry, and thinly slice the potatoes.
  2. Place in a bowl with salt.
  3. Heat up the olive oil in a deep pan, and cook the potatoes for about 20mins. They should be slightly golden.
  4. Scoop up the potatoes with as little oil as possible and place them into the bowl with the beaten eggs.
  5. Once mixed through and seasoned, pour the mixture in a small round pan with a little of the leftover oil.
  6. Cook for about 3mins each side, using a plate to help flip the tortilla.
