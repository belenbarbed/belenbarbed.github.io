Gooey Brownies

Ingredients (for 6):

  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 100g dark chocolate, for melting
  • 50g plain flour
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate, for chopping
  • 50g dark chocolate, for chopping
  • 2 eggs
  • 200g golden caster sugar

Method:

  1. Melt the dark chocolate and butter in a bain marie, leave to cool.
  2. Sift the flour, baking powder, and cocoa powder into a bowl.
  3. Whisk the eggs and sugar until pale and double in size.
  4. Getly fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mousse.
  5. Sift the dry ingredients into the mix and fold gently.
  6. Stir in the chopped white and dark chocolate chunks.
  7. Pour the brownie mixture into a greased tin.
  8. Cook for 20 mins at 160ºC fan, until the centre isn't wobbly.
  9. Set aside to cool before serving.
