Black Bean Chilli Stir-Fry

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2 egg noodle nests
  • Some stir-fry veggies
  • 2tbsp Lao Gan Ma black beans in chilli oil
  • 2tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 2tbsp water
  • 1tsp chicken broth powder

Method:

  1. Add all the sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix them well.
  2. Stir fry the veggies while the noodles cook.
  3. Once, the veggies are done, add the noodles in.
  4. When that's all combined, add the sauce mix and mix it through.
