Veggie Tikka Masala - source

Ingredients (for 4):

  • 1 cauliflower head
  • 1 big carrot
  • 2 onions
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 500ml of prepared Tikka Masala sauce (by Sainsbury's - 1 bottle)
  • 1 block of paneer (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of cornflour
  • Salt, pepper, cumin, coriander, tumeric

Method:

  1. Cut the cauliflower into small bite-sized florets and wash.
  2. Chop and microwave the carrot for 2-3 mins to soften them.
  3. Cut the onions into thin strips.
  4. Cook the cauliflower, onions, carrot, cubed panner, garlic and ginger all together in a big pot with plenty of oil.
  5. Once the onions are soft and almost transparent, add the tikka masala sauce, tomato paste, and extra water to cover all the ingredients.
  6. Cover and let cook for 10-15 mins.
  7. Remove the cover and add the cornflower and extra spices to taste.
  8. Cook for a little longer until it has the desired thickness.
  9. Serve with rice or naan.
