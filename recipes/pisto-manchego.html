Pisto Manchego - source

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 100ml tomato sauce
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • salt, pepper and olive oil

Method:

  1. Cook the crushed and finely chopped garlic in plenty of olive oil. Never stop stirring.
  2. Add the chopped onion and peppers. Cook until softened (adding salt to help).
  3. Add the chopped courgette and poach everything until soft.
  4. Add the tomatoes, peeled and de-seeded. Once they're soft you can chop them with your tongs/spatula.
  5. Once all the veggies are done, add a bit of tomato sauce. Stir until hot throughout.
  6. Serve with eggs in any form (fried, poached, omelet...).
