Tomato and Egg-drop Soup - source

Ingredients (for 1):

  • 1 green onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 400ml chicken broth
  • 1 pack of instant noodles
  • chili oil

Method:

  1. Chop the green onion and seperate whites from greens.
  2. Smash and mince the garlic and chop the tomatoes.
  3. Heat up oil in a pot and fry the white parts of the green onion until soft, then add the garlic.
  4. Get it nice and warm then add the tomatoes, salt, sugar, and white pepper.
  5. Cook until the tomatoes have released liquid and are soft, then add chicken broth.
  6. Once it's boiling again, add the noodles into the soup.
  7. 1 min before the noodles are done, reduce heat on the soup and add the whisked egg.
  8. Top with the green part of the green onion and chili oil
