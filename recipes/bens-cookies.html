Ben's Cookies

Ingredients (for 12 cookies):

  • 180g plain flour
  • 20g baking powder
  • 85g unsalted butter
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • Milk chocolate chips, or equivalent
  • A sprinkle of salt

Method:

  1. Melt the butter in a bowl, and stir in the sugars and vanilla extract.
  2. Making sure it's not to hot, crack the egg in and keep mixing.
  3. Pour into a larger bowl, where the flour, baking powder, and salt has been mixed.
  4. Stir well until fully combined. This will make a stiff dough.
  5. Place one spoonful of dough on the baking sheet at a time, leaving plenty of room in between them.
  6. Place the chocolate chips on each dough ball.
  7. Bake at 180ºC for 10 mins.
