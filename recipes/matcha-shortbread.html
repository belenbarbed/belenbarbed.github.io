Matcha Shortbread

Ingredients (for 20 cookies):

  • 240g flour
  • 10g matcha powder
  • 90g unsalted butter (softened)
  • 70g powder sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 30g white chocolate
  • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Sift the matcha powder and flour into a bowl.
  2. With a hand mixer, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and the sugar, and keep mixing until light.
  4. Add the egg yolks and continue mixing.
  5. Slowly incorporate the flour mixture while still mixing, until a smooth dough is formed.
  6. Add the blitzed white chocolate to the dough.
  7. Shape the dough into a cilinder, cling-wrap tight and store in the fridge for at least 2h (preferably overnight).
  8. Once cold, cut into 1cm thick cookies.
  9. Bake for 15mins at 150ºC fan. Let cool.
