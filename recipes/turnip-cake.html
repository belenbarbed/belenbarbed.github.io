Turnip Cake

Ingredients (for 3 small cakes (~xcm diameter)):

  • 1.2kg Daikon radish
  • 2 cured Chinese sausages
  • 30g dried shrimp
  • 200g rice flour
  • 70g wheat flour
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2tsp chicken powder
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Method:

  1. Soak the dried shrimps for at least 1h
  2. Boil the grease and wax off the sausages, then peel and finely chop.
  3. Stir fry the shrimps, setting aside their water, until aromatic.
  4. Add in the sausages until aromatic, then set aside.
  5. Peel the radish until you see the netting, keeping about 80%.
  6. Cut into chuncks, then grate it into a bowl.
  7. Using your hands, pick up the solid pieces of radish, squeezing the juice out of them back into the bowl, setting the dried radish aside.
  8. Add the flours and shrimp water into the radish juice, and mix until it becomes a batter.
  9. Sautee the dry radish in a wok, and season with the salt, pepper, sugar, and chicken powder.
  10. Add 3/4 of the meat and shrimp, and continue cooking.
  11. Set to low heat, and add the batter in.
  12. Cook until the dough has reduced and become sticky.
  13. Place in oiled trays, and steam for 45 mins or until an inserted chopstick comes out clean.
  14. Cut into pieces and stir fry, serve with the remaining sausage and shrimp on top. Hoisin or sweet chilli sauce pairs well.
