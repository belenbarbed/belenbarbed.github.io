Lemon Curd Tarts

Ingredients (for 8 tarts):

  • 2 lemons
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 150g brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 85g golden syrup
  • 4tbsp vegetable oil
  • 150g plain flour
  • 2tsp baking powder

Method:

  1. For the tarts, mix the golden syrup and vegetable oil until combined.
  2. Add the flour and baking powder, mix, and then knead by hand until the dough stops falling apart. Rest for 1h.
  3. Divide the dough into 8 pieces, and line a muffin tray with the flattened dough.
  4. Line each tart with some kitchen foil and pour some rice/beans on top to prevent the bottom from rising.
  5. Bake at 200ºC for 10 mins, then for an extra 5 without the foil. Set aside.
  6. For the curd, zest the lemons and blend the zest with the sugar until finely minced.
  7. In a bowl, cream the room-temperature butter with the zest and sugar mixture.
  8. Add the eggs, one at a time, and then the juice from the lemons. Mix well.
  9. Pour the mixture into a pan, and cook over low heat until thickened.
  10. Pour the curd into the tart shells and allow to cool fully before serving.
