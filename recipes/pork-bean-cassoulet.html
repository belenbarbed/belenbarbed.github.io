Pork and Butterbean Cassoulet

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2-4 pork medallions (depending on size)
  • 400g butterbeans (1 can)
  • ??g shallots
  • ??g mushrooms
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 100g spinach
  • 2tbsp flour
  • 400ml chicken stock
  • 100ml cream
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, thyme

Method:

  1. Fry off the medallions in some olive oil until brown (but not cooked through), set aside.
  2. In the resulting oil, fry the chopped shallots, mushrooms, and garlic.
  3. After 3-4mins, add the strained beans, as well as the flour, and mix well.
  4. Slowly incorporate the stock, making sure it's well combined before adding more.
  5. Once it's simmering, add the pork back in, cover, and simmer for 10mins.
  6. Finally, add the cream and spinach. Serve once the latter has wilted.
