Broccoli and Beef in Oyster Sauce - source

Ingredients (for 2):

  • One head of broccoli
  • One tender beef steak
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Oyster sauce
  • Cornflour

Method:

  1. Cut the trees out of the broccoli head and boil for 3-4mins.
  2. Fry the chopped garlic in sesame oil.
  3. Add the thinly sliced steak and continue to stir-fry.
  4. Once it's almost cooked through, add a good amount of oyster sauce and a bit of water.
  5. Add the boiled broccoli and let the sauce thicken (cornflour can help).
  6. Serve with rice.
