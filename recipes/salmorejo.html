Salmorejo Cordobes

Ingredients (for 1):

  • 250g ripe tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 toasted bread slice
  • 50ml olive oil
  • Salt, pepper

Method:

  1. Blend the diced tomatoes to a fine soup.
  2. Strain the soup into a bowl, and let the bread soak in it for at least 10mins.
  3. Blend the soup with the bread together with the peeled garlic cloves and the olive oil. It should turn orange after a while.
  4. Season and refrigerate well before serving.
