German Salad - source

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 2 large Frankfurters
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 spoonfuls of mayo
  • 1 teaspoon of dijon mustard
  • A bit of parsley

Method:

  1. Boil the diced potatoes in salty water for 30 mins. Salt and pepper them.
  2. Finely chop the onion and add to the potatoes.
  3. Fry off the sausages for a couple of minutes, then chop and add.
  4. Mix the mayo, mustard and parsley. Add.
