Croquetas de Bacalao

Ingredients (for approx. 24 croquetas):

  • 200g cod fillet(s)
  • 60g butter
  • 60g flour
  • 400ml milk
  • 2-3 eggs
  • breadcrumbs
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Method:

  1. Steam the cod fillets until fully cooked, around 10-15 mins.
  2. Shred the cod meat and set aside.
  3. In a pan, melt the butter, and whisk in the flour until homogeneous.
  4. Fry off for a couple of minutes, then slowly add the milk bit by bit until fully integrated.
  5. Season and cook for 10 mins, then add the cod.
  6. Cook for a further 5 mins. Place in a bowl, with clingfilm touching the entire mixture, and let cool at least overnight in the fridge.
  7. Using two teaspoons, form the dough into balls.
  8. Cover in breadcrumbs, egg, and breadcrumbs again.
  9. Fry the croquetas in olive oil deep enough to cover them entirely.
  10. Dry the excess oil with a paper towel, and serve.
