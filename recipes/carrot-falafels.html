Carrot Falafels

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 1 can of chickpeas (400g)
  • 100g-200g of carrots
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 garlic clove

Method:

  1. Drain the chickpeas with a tea towel.
  2. Peel and thinly cut the carrots.
  3. Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend together to form a paste.
  4. Form paste into small balls.
  5. Fry off in a pan with oil.
