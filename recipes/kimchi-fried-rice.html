Kimchi Fried Rice - source

Ingredients (for 1):

  • 1 cup of kimchi
  • 15ml Gochujang
  • 10ml soy sauce
  • 10ml sesame oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 green onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 portion of rice (cooked)

Method:

  1. Squeeze all the juice out of the kimchi and set aside.
  2. Chop the now dry kimchi, as well as the onion and garlic.
  3. Mix the gochujang and soy sauce with the kimchi juice.
  4. Drizzle the sesame oil into the coocked rice.
  5. Stir fry the onions and garlic, one softened add the kimchi too.
  6. Add the rice and stir well to avoid clumps.
  7. Add the sauce mix in and stir fry for a while longer.
  8. Add the eggs, and cook them in with the residual heat.
  9. Serve with the chopped green onion on top.
