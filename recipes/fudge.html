Fudge

Ingredients (for ?? g):

  • 400g (one can) condensed milk
  • 150ml milk
  • 450g brown sugar
  • 80g butter

Method:

  1. Place all the ingredients in a large non-stick pan.
  2. Melt over low heat, until the sugar fully dissolves.
  3. Bring to a steady boil for 10-15 mins, stirring continuously to prevent it from burning.
  4. Remove from the heat and let cool for 5 mins.
  5. Beat the mixture until it just looses it's shine and becomes very thick, for about 10 mins.
  6. Press into a tin and leave to set before cutting up into cubes.
