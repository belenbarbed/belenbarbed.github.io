Pimientos Rellenos

Ingredients (for 3):

  • 15 whole pimientos del piquillo
  • 250g of pork mince
  • 250g of beef mince
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 2 heaped tbsp of flour
  • 150ml tomato sauce
  • Milk
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 500ml beef stock
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, paprika

Method:

  1. Start cooking the onion sauce by thinly slicing the onion and garlic, and frying it off in some olive oil.
  2. Once they're turning golden, add a heaped tbsp of flour and stir well.
  3. Add the stock and bring to a boil for 5mins.
  4. Blend the sauce in the pan, and leave on low heat to reduce.
  5. In the meantime, cook the mince meat in a pan, stirring often to separate the chunks.
  6. Once it's all cooked, add the tomato sauce, grated egg, and season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  7. Add a heaped tbsp of flour and stir until fully combined.
  8. Incorporate the milk slowly, until the mixture has lightened in colour and cannot absorb any more.
  9. Simmer for a couple more minutes, then set aside to cool.
  10. Once cooled, stuff all the peppers to the brim with the meat filling.
  11. Add the now-reduced onion sauce to the pan that has the excess filling, and place the stuffed peppers on top.
  12. Simmer it all gently for 10-15mins.
