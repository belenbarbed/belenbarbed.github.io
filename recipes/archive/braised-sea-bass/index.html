Braised Sea Bass

Ingredients (for 2):

  • One sea bass
  • Soy sauce
  • Chinese vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green onions
  • A bit of water

Method:

  1. Clean the fish, make 3-4 cuts on both sides of the fish and cover with cornflour all around.
  2. Infuse the pan oil with some ginger and fry the fish until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Take the fish out while you make the sauce in the same pot by mixing all the ingredients, then add the fish back.
  4. Cook for another 10-15mins, until most of the liquid has been absorbed.
