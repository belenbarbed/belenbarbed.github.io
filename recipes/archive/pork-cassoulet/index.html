One-pot Pork Medallions and Apricot Cassoulet - source

Ingedients (for 2):

  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 30ml of oil
  • 4 pork medallions
  • 2 sprigs of thyme
  • 400g tinned butterbeans
  • 10ml of plain flour
  • 8 prunes
  • 400ml chicken stock
  • 4 apricots
  • 150g spinach
  • 30ml of double cream

Method:

  1. Cook the seasoned pork in the oil until brown. Set aside.
  2. In the same oil, add the halved shallots, crushed garlic, and thyme. Fry for 3-4mins.
  3. Add the beans and flour and toss everything together.
  4. Pour the stock little by little to combine it well.
  5. Return the pork to the pan, add the de-stoned prunes and simmer for 10 mins with a lid.
  6. Add the spinach and de-stoned and quartered apricots. Put the lid on again to wilt.
  7. Finish by stirring all together with the double cream.
