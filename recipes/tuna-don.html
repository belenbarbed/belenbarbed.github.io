Tuna Mayo Don

Ingredients (for 1):

  • 1 can tuna
  • 1 tbsp kewpie mayo
  • wasabi, to taste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 green onion
  • 1/2 a ripe avocado
  • shichimi togarashi
  • shredded yaki nori (toasted seaweed)

Method:

  1. Mix the tuna, mayo, wasabi, and soy sauce in a bowl.
  2. Slice the avocado and place in a bowl on top of rice.
  3. Add the tuna mayo mixture and garnish with the chopped green onion, shichimi togarashi, and nori.
