Japanese Curry

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 1 onion (~225g)
  • 2 carrots (~65g)
  • 1 potato (~75g)
  • 250g protein (mince, tofu...)
  • 2 blocks of Golden Curry sauce
  • 330ml water
  • Vegetable oil

Method:

  1. Chop all the vegetables into small cubes.
  2. Stir fry the vegetables with the protein for about 5mins in some vegetable oil.
  3. Add the water and let it simmer for 15mins.
  4. Add the curry cubes and stir them in well.
  5. Let it simmer for another 5mins.
  6. Serve on rice or noodles.
