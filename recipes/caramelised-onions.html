Caramelised Onions

Ingredients (for a couple of servings):

  • 3 red onions
  • 30ml olive oil
  • 30ml brown sugar
  • 30ml balsamic vinegar
  • Salt

Method:

  1. Chop all the onions into half-rings.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan and slowly cook the onions with some salt until they're soft.
  3. Add the sugar and vinegar and cook further until fully caramelised.
