Torrijas

Ingredients (for one bagguette's worth):

  • 1 day-old bagguette
  • 500ml milk
  • Peel of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g sugar, for the coating
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon, for the coating
  • Olive oil

Method:

  1. Warn up the milk in a pot with the lemon, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Once it starts boiling, remove from the heat and let it cool with the flavours.
  3. Strain the milk into a tray, and let the slices of bread sit in it until fully soaked and softened, while still holding their shape.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl, and add a couple of tablespoons of the milk into the mix.
  5. Coat the bread pieces in the egg and then fry in some olive oil.
  6. Once fried, let rest on some kitchen paper, then coat with the remaining sugar and cinnamon.
