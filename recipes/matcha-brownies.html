Matcha Brownies

Ingredients (for 15 squares):

  • 160g white chocolate, to melt
  • 40g white chocolate, to stir in
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 3tbsp matcha
  • 70g granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 80g flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Melt the white chocolate and the butter together in a bain-marie.
  2. Add the sugar, then the eggs one by one, stirring until smooth in between.
  3. Add the matcha, flour, baking powder, and salt, mixing until completely distributed.
  4. Stir in the chopped white chocolate, and spread the mixture onto a greased tray.
  5. Bake for 12mins at 180ºC.
  6. Let cool in the refrigerator before serving.
