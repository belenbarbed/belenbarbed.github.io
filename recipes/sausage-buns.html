Chinese sausage buns

Ingredients (for 6 buns):

  • 3 cured Chinese sausages (Lop Cheung)
  • 250g plain flour
  • 150ml milk
  • 1/2 pack instant yeast (7g)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Method:

  1. In a stand mixer, mix all the ingredients for 9-12 minutes with hook and low speed until get a elastic, smooth and soft dough.
  2. Shape it to a large ball and cover with wet cloth. Set aside for 1h.
  3. Re-knead in the stand mixer slowly for 2mins, until the dough is smooth again.
  4. Divide the dough into 6 portions and then shape each one into a long strip (around 30cm long with smaller ends).
  5. Wrap the strips around the half sausages, and rest on some parchment paper for 20mins, then steam for another 20mins.
