Chicken Pesto and Courgette Pasta - source

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 3 courgettes
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Pesto sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarela, goat's)
  • Salt, pepper, oil

Method:

  1. With a vegetable peeler, shave ribbons of the zucchini until you reach the seeds.
  2. Fry off the chicken cut into strips/chunks.
  3. Once the chicken is fully cooked, add the pesto, some more olive oil and the courgette ribbons.
  4. Reduce the heat, add the cheese, and stir until all is warm through.
