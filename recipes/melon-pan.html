Melon Pan

Ingredients (for 5-6 buns):

  • 90g heavy cream
  • 120g milk
  • 1/2 egg
  • 35g granulated sugar
  • 275g plain flour
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 pack of active dry yeast
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 2tbsp matcha powder, optional
  • a pinch of salt
  • 42g unsalted butter, for the topping
  • 35g icing sugar, for the topping
  • 40g granulated sugar, for the topping
  • 85g plain flour, for the topping
  • 1 egg white, for the topping
  • 1tsp vanilla extract, for the topping
  • a pinch of salt, for the topping
  • 1tbsp matcha powder, optional for the topping
  • optional filling, such as chocolate or sweet red bean paste

Method:

  1. Warm the cream on medium heat and dissolve 25g of the plain flour with a spatula.
  2. Once homogeneous, add into the stand mixer bowl, together with the rest of the main dough ingredients.
  3. Mix with the dough hook for 15mins, then cover and let rest for 1h.
  4. Make the topping dough by creaming the butter and both sugars. Then add the egg white, vanilla, and salt.
  5. Gently incorporate the flour, and refrigerate for 1h. Before using, knead with the dough hook for 5mins.
  6. Divide the main dough into 5-6 balls, with the optional filling wrapped inside.
  7. With floured hands, divide the topping dough and roll it out to lay it over the buns, tucking in the excess.
  8. Let them proof for an extra 1h, and then score the tops with a criss-cross pattern using a knife.
  9. Bake for 20mins at 175ºC.
