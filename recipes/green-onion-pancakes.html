Green Onion Pancakes

Ingredients (for 5 pancakes):

  • 120g flour
  • 200ml water
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 green onions

Method:

  1. Add all the ingredients in a bowl - including the minced garlic and chopped green onions.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Oil a non-stick pan and cook a ladle-full of the batter at a time.
  4. Try to flatten the batter as much as possible.
  5. Cook for ~3mins each side or until golden brown.
  6. Serve with a soy sauce and black vinegar dip.
