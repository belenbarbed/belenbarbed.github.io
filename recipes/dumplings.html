Jiu Jiu's Dumplings

Ingredients (for 30-35 dumplings):

  • 250g minced pork
  • 125g shrimp
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 thumb of ginger
  • 1 large spring onion
  • 1 Egg
  • 5-6 dehydrated shiitake mushrooms
  • Shiitake mushroom juice
  • Salt
  • White pepper
  • Chinese 5 spice (optional)
  • Sesame oil
  • Plain flour, for the dough
  • Lukewarm water, for the dough

Method:

  1. Make approximately 650g of dough, it should be stiff and dry. Rest for 30 mins.
  2. Mix all the filling ingredients in a bowl, finely minced.
  3. Stir the bowl thoroughly, always in the same direction (eg. anti-clockwise).
  4. Separate the dough into two parts, and roll into a long tube, then cut into pieces.
  5. Roll out each piece into a disk using a rolling pin, coating both sides in plenty of flour.
  6. Fill and fold each disk into dumplings.
  7. Freeze immediately unless you plan to cook them on the day.
  8. If boiling, boil until they float, then add cold water until it boils again. Repeat two more times.
  9. Alternatively, you can pan fry until the bottom is crispy, then add water up to 1/3rd of their height and steam until fully evaporated.
