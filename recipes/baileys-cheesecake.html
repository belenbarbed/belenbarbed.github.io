Baileys Cheesecake

Ingredients (for 8):

  • 1 tub of philadelphia (180g)
  • 200ml double cream
  • 1 tube of oreos (150g)
  • 100g of unsalted butter
  • 2 shots of Bailey's
  • 6 squares of chocolate (eg. darkmilk)
  • Sweetener

Method:

  1. Remove the filling from the oreos and set aside
  2. Blitz the oreo cookies and mix with the melted butter
  3. Pat a layer of the cookie mix into each container, and chill
  4. While it is chilling, melt the chocolate.
  5. In a bowl, whip the cream until it forms soft peaks.
  6. In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese until it is soft.
  7. Fold in the whipped cream, Bailey's and three-quarters of the chocolate.
  8. When the mixture is smooth, pour it evenly onto the biscuit base.
  9. Smooth the remaining chocolate on top of the cheesecake mixture.
  10. Refridgerate and allow to set for at least 2h, preferably overnight.
