Nametake

Ingredients (for 2):

  • 200g of enoki mushrooms (1 bag)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar

Method:

  1. Cut off the brown end of the Enoki mushrooms and wash.
  2. Put in a pot all the ingredients except the vinegar and cook at medium heat, stirring constantly.
  3. When thickened, add the vinegar and cook 2-3 minutes longer.
  4. Remove from heat, and let cool.
