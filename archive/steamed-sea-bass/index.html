Steamed Sea Bass

Ingredients (for 2):

  • One sea bass
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green onions
  • Fish soy sauce
  • Sichuan peppercorns

Method:

  1. Make 3-4 cuts on both sides of the fish and place in a microwave-safe pot.
  2. Add ginger, garlic, green onion and the fish soy sauce to the pot.
  3. Put the container in the microwave for 4-5mins at the highest setting.
  4. Meanwhile, heat up some oil in a pan with Sichuan peppercorns and let it get really hot.
  5. When the microwave has finished, pour the oil over the fish and serve.
